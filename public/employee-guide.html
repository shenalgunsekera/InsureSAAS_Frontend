Logging In

First-time and daily sign-in steps

1

Get your credentials

Your manager will give you an email address and password. These are your login credentials for the portal. Keep them secure and do not share them.

2

Open the portal

Navigate to insuresaas-frontend.vercel.app in your browser (Chrome or Edge recommended). Bookmark it for quick access.

3

Enter your credentials

Type your email and password on the login screen, then click Sign In. You'll be taken directly to the client dashboard.

⚠ Security: Never share your password with anyone, including your manager. If you think your account is compromised, raise a Support Ticket immediately.
2

Understanding the Dashboard

What you see after logging in

1

The Sidebar

On the left is the navigation sidebar. You'll see: Clients (main list), Reports (financial summaries), and Raise a Ticket at the bottom for support requests.

2

Summary Cards

At the top of the Clients page are four summary cards: Total Clients, Expiring Soon (within 30 days), Expired, and Total Net Premium. These update in real-time as you add or modify clients.

3

The Client Table

Below the cards is the main client table. Each row shows a client's name, phone, product, policy status, expiry date, and net premium. You can search and filter this list.

💡 Tip: Use the search bar at the top of the page to instantly find a client by name, mobile number, policy number, or email.

Adding a New Client

1

Click "Add Client"

Click the orange + Add Client button at the top-right of the client list. A form panel will open on the right side of the screen.

2

3

Upload documents (optional at this stage)

You can upload documents now or come back to edit and add them later. See Section 4 for upload details.

4

Click "Save Client"

After filling the form, click the Save Client button. Your submission is sent to your manager for approval. It will appear in your list with a Pending approval label.

📋 Note: Your submission goes to a manager for review before it becomes a live record. You'll see the status change to Approved once it's confirmed.

Uploading Documents

Attaching PDFs and images to a client record

1

Supported document types

You can upload PDFs, JPGs, and PNGs. The portal supports these document categories for each client:

  • Policyholder Document
  • Proposal Form
  • Quotation
  • CR Copy
  • Schedule
  • Invoice / Debit Note
  • Payment Receipt
  • NIC / BR Document
2

How to upload

In the client form (Add or Edit), scroll to the Documents section at the top. Click any document box or drag and drop a file onto it. You'll see an upload progress bar — wait until it shows a green tick before saving.

3

Viewing uploaded documents

Click the eye icon on any client row to open the details panel, then click the Documents tab. Each uploaded document has a View link that opens it in your browser.

⚠ File size: Maximum file size is 20 MB per document. If your file is larger, please compress it before uploading.
💡 Tip: You can update documents at any time by clicking the Edit (pencil) icon on a client row and uploading a new file over the existing one.

Approval Workflow

What happens after you submit a client

1

Submitted → Pending

When you save a new client, it appears in your list with a Pending approval badge. It is not yet visible to other employees — only you and managers can see it at this stage.

2

Manager reviews it

Your manager sees all pending submissions in the Admin Panel. They will review the details and either approve or reject the record.

3

Approved

If approved, the Pending badge disappears and the client becomes a live record visible to everyone. No further action needed from you.

4

Rejected

If rejected, the client shows a Rejected badge in your list. Hover over the badge to see the rejection reason. Edit the client to fix the issue and re-submit — your manager will then see it again for approval.

Viewing & Searching Clients

Finding and reviewing client records

1

Search

Use the Search bar at the top of the page. It searches across client name, mobile number, policy number, product, and email — instantly as you type.

2

Filter by type

Use the Customer Type dropdown to filter clients by Individual or Company.

3

View full details

Click the eye icon (👁) on any row to open the full client details panel. It has tabs for General, Address, Contact, Policy, Financials, and Documents.

4

Policy expiry colours

Each client row shows a coloured status chip: Active (policy is current), Expiring (within 30 days), and Expired (policy has lapsed).

7

Raising a Support Ticket

Reporting issues or requesting changes

1

Click "Raise a Ticket" in the sidebar

At the bottom of the left sidebar, click Raise a Ticket. A form dialog will appear.

2

Fill in the ticket details

Provide a clear Subject (e.g. "Cannot upload PDF for client X"), select the Category (Bug Report, Feature Request, etc.) and Priority (Low, Medium, High, Critical).

3

Write a detailed description

In the Description field, explain the issue clearly: what you were trying to do, what happened, and any error messages you saw. The more detail, the faster it gets resolved.

4

Submit

Click Submit Ticket. Your manager will see it in the Admin Panel and respond with a status update and notes.

💡 Use tickets for: Bugs / errors, feature requests, access issues, data corrections, anything that needs admin attention.

FAQ & Tips

Common questions and best practices

Q

My client submission was rejected — what do I do?

Hover over the Rejected badge to see the reason. Click the edit (pencil) icon, fix the flagged information, and save again. It will go back into the approval queue automatically.

Q

Why can't I see a client I added?

Newly submitted clients show in your personal list with a Pending badge while awaiting manager approval. They won't appear in the main list for other employees until approved.

Q

The document upload is slow — is that normal?

Upload speed depends on your internet connection and file size. For large PDFs, it can take 30–60 seconds. The progress bar shows exactly how far along the upload is. Do not close the browser tab until the upload completes.

Q

I can't log in — what do I do?

Check your email and password. Passwords are case-sensitive. If you've forgotten your password, raise a support ticket or contact your manager to have your account reset.

Q

Can I edit a client after submitting?

Yes — click the pencil icon on any client row (including pending ones) to open the edit form. Saving edits to a pending client will keep it in the pending queue for manager review.

🔒 Best practices: Always log out when using a shared computer (click your name → sign out at the bottom of the sidebar). Keep your password secret. Report suspicious activity immediately via a support ticket.
