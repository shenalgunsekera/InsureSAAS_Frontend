What's Included in Every Plan
📋

Quotations Module

Fire, Motor, Life, Marine, Medical & Engineering product forms. Send to multiple insurers, track responses in real-time, compare side-by-side.

📝

Underwriting

Full client record management, document uploads to Cloudinary, approval workflow, policy tracking, and search/filter.

🔄

Renewals Tracker

Real-time expiry countdown for all policies. Colour-coded urgency — due in 7, 30, 60 days, and expired.

📊

Reports & Analytics

Financial summaries, portfolio breakdown, expiry reports. Export to premium branded Excel with logo.

🏠

Operational Menu

Clean module-based portal with role-based access. Each staff member only sees what they need.

🔒

Security Built-in

20-minute inactivity auto-logout, session-only persistence (closed tab = signed out), role-based permissions.

📧

Automated Emails

Quote requests sent automatically to insurers with a branded email and unique response link. Customer comparison emails included.

🤝

Support Ticket System

Staff can raise support tickets from the dashboard. Manager reviews, responds, and updates status — fully tracked.

☁️

Cloud Native

Built on Google Firebase / Microsoft Azure. No servers to manage, automatic scaling, data stored securely in the cloud.
