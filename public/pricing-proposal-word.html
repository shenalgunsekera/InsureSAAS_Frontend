Terms & Conditions

1. Pricing & Payment
All prices are in Sri Lankan Rupees (LKR) and quoted on a monthly subscription basis, billed in advance. Infrastructure costs (Firebase, Azure, Vercel, SendGrid, Cloudinary) are estimates based on projected usage and are billed at actual cost with no markup. Prices are valid for 30 days from the date of issue.

2. Setup
A one-time setup and deployment fee may apply depending on scope. This will be confirmed separately before commencement.

3. Contract Term
Minimum commitment of 3 months from the date of first invoice. A 30-day written notice is required for cancellation after the minimum term.

4. Intellectual Property
The source code and system architecture remain the intellectual property of Shenal Gunasekara. The client receives a perpetual, non-transferable licence to use the deployed system for internal business operations. The licence is valid for as long as the monthly subscription is active.

5. Scope of Maintenance
The monthly maintenance fee covers bug fixes, security patches, performance improvements, and minor feature enhancements. Major new feature development is scoped and quoted separately.

6. Data Ownership
All client data entered into the system remains the sole property of InsureSAAS Ltd. Data is stored on Google Firebase / Microsoft Azure infrastructure with industry-standard encryption.
